My 1986 Porsche 944

This love story is no like others, this is love like no other; between a man and his car. I bought this car May 8, 2020 for $5000. May 8th is now a more exciting day for me then my birthday. This car and i have been through a lot. Countless parts went into to it get it back into its prime running condition. I'll never forget the first time i layed my eyes on this beauty, my eyes lit up brighter then fireworks on New Years Eve.
